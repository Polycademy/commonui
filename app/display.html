Display Positioning with Z-index Demos

Note! This demo requires you to play around with the settings to see how the display, positioning and z-index properties work. You'll notice that we have an horizontal scrollbar here. This is because we're using block level elements, and the positioning rules will affect their whole width. We would need to either give them a specific smaller width, or make them display: inline-block or display:inline, both works, but inline-block is more appropriate for images. Use firebug or your web developer tools to see the layout as you change the settings.
